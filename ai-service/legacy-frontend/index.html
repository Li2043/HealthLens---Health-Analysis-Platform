Safe AI Consultation Demo

HealthLens

A safe AI healthcare consultation workflow

HealthLens explores how an LLM can support safer healthcare consultations through a structured workflow: symptom signal extraction, risk assessment, emergency escalation, and safety-validated explanation.

This tool is a software demonstration only. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment guidance. If symptoms are severe, urgent, or worsening, seek professional medical help.

Product overview

What HealthLens does

HealthLens turns a short, free-text health note into a structured, safety-managed consultation. Instead of a single chatbot reply, it runs a designed workflow that extracts signals, assesses risk, decides whether to escalate, and generates a calm, non-diagnostic explanation — with safety checks at every step.

  • Structured, not a chatbot. The LLM is one constrained component inside a transparent workflow.
  • Safety-first. No diagnosis, no medication advice, always a clear next step.
  • Risk-aware. Behaviour changes across four triage tiers, with decisive emergency escalation.

Risk communication

How risk is communicated

Low

Calm, honest reassurance. Self-monitor and seek advice if it persists.

Moderate

Names the contributing signals. Consider contacting a professional.

High

Serious and direct. Recommends prompt professional care.

Emergency

Leads with urgent escalation. Overrides risk on red-flag symptoms.

Safety strategy

Guardrails built into every response

  • Scope & uncertainty. Detects health vs. non-health input and preserves uncertainty instead of guessing.
  • No diagnosis or dosing. A prompt + output validator block diagnostic claims and medication advice.
  • Risk-aware responses. Tone and next step change with the triage tier.
  • Emergency escalation. Red-flag symptoms trigger an unconditional "seek urgent care" message.
  • Human referral. High-risk and uncertain cases are steered toward a professional.
